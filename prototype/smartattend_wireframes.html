Note: Screens 1 and 2 are intentionally separate flows. The NFC Kiosk (Screen 1) is the physical attendance-capture device at the classroom door — students just tap their card, no login required. The Portal Login (Screen 2) is only for people accessing the web dashboards (viewing history, monitoring live sessions, managing users) — a different context, on a browser or phone.
